Welcome to the ESPA Ordering Interface

This is the LDCM Science R&D (LSRD) ESPA Ordering Interface, allowing you to upload scenes and request any available level of processing free of charge. Keep in mind that all processing provided by LSRD is strictly for evaluation purposes only: The processing outputs may be considered possible candidates for standard products in the future.

Current available processing includes surface reflectance for applicable Landsat 5 TM and 7 ETM+ via Ledaps. In the future, additional processing will be available for evaluation including: T1 - River Discharge, T2 - Water Use, T3 - Ground Water, T4 - Lake Levels, T5 - Snow Cover, T6 - Glaciers and Ice Caps, T7 - Permafrost and Seasonally Frozen Ground, T8 - Albedo, T9 - Land Cover, T10 - fAPAR, T11 - Leaf Area Index, T12 - Biomass, T13 - Fire Disturbance, and finally Soil Moisture (Emerging ECV).

Land Surface Temperature is another Climate Data Record planned to be offered through ESPA
